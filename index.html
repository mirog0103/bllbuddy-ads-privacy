B$LL Buddy – Ads & Data Privacy

Effective: 30 Aug 2025 • Contact: mirog0103@gmail.com

This page explains how B$LL Buddy (“we”, “us”) uses advertising technologies (Google AdMob and mediation partners) in our iOS app. It supplements our main Privacy Policy and focuses on ad-related data, consent, and your choices.

We do not access your Contacts, Camera/Photos, or precise (GPS) location. Ad platforms may use approximate location (e.g., city/region) from network signals for things like geo-targeting and fraud prevention.

What data may be used for ads

  • Ad performance & interactions (impressions, taps, frequency capping, session info).
  • Approximate location inferred by providers (no GPS collected by us).
  • Device & app info (model, OS version, language, app version, IP for security/fraud control).
  • Identifiers for ads (e.g., IDFA if you consent and allow tracking under iOS ATT; otherwise limited/none).

Who processes ad data

We serve ads via Google AdMob and may request ads through mediation. Partners operate under their own privacy terms:

  • Google AdMob – policies.google.com/privacy
  • AppLovin (MAX) – applovin.com/privacy/
  • Unity Ads – unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • ironSource (LevelPlay) – is.com/privacy-policy/
  • Meta (Audience Network) – facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • Vungle – vungle.com/privacy/
  • InMobi – inmobi.com/privacy-policy/
  • AdColony – adcolony.com/privacy-policy/

Note: The active set of mediation partners may change over time (e.g., by region or auction). We will update this list periodically.

Legal basis & regional info

EEA/UK (GDPR/UK GDPR): We rely on your consent to process personal data for personalized ads via Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP). If you decline consent, we show non-personalized or contextual ads when available.
US (CPRA): We honor “Do Not Sell or Share” choices using AdMob/UMP privacy controls. Your device’s iOS settings can also limit tracking.

Change / revoke your choice

You can change or revoke your ads consent at any time:

  1. Recommended: Tap the button below to open B$LL Buddy and show the consent options form.
  2. If the app doesn’t open, open B$LL Buddy → Prfile → Manage Ads Consent.
  3. Additionally, on iOS you can manage tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Change / Revoke Consent in App Didn’t open? See instructions

If the app didn’t open automatically, open B$LL Buddy on your device, go to Settings → Privacy → Manage Ads Consent and follow the prompts.

Your other choices

  • Limit Ad Tracking: iOS → Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Reset Advertising ID: iOS → Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking (toggle permissions as needed).
  • Apple Advertising: iOS → Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

Data retention

Ad vendors retain data under their policies for reporting, fraud prevention, and compliance. B$LL Buddy does not store raw advertising identifiers in its own databases.

Children

B$LL Buddy is not directed to children under 13 (or the local age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Updates

We may update this page as partners, settings, or laws change. We will adjust the effective date above and publish the new version here.
