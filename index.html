B$LL Buddy – Ads & Data Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 Nov 2024 • Contact: mirog0103@gmail.com

This page explains how B$LL Buddy (“we”, “us”) uses advertising technologies (Google AdMob and selected mediation partners) in the iOS app. It supplements our main Privacy Policy and focuses on ad-related data.

What we collect for ads

  • Ad performance and interaction (e.g., impressions, taps, frequency capping).
  • Approximate location (city/region) inferred by the ad provider (we do not collect GPS).
  • Device and app info (app version, OS version, model, language, IP for fraud-prevention).
  • Identifiers used for ads (e.g., IDFA if you consent / ATT allows it; otherwise limited or none).

We do not access or collect your Contacts, Camera, Photos, or precise (GPS) location.

Who processes ad data

We serve ads via Google AdMob and may use mediation to request ads from multiple ad networks. Each partner acts as its own controller/processor of ad data under its policies.

  • Google AdMob / Google Marketing Platform – Privacy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • AppLovin (MAX) – Privacy: applovin.com/privacy/
  • Unity – Privacy: unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • ironSource (LevelPlay) – Privacy: is.com/privacy-policy/

Legal basis & region-specific info

In the EEA/UK, we rely on your consent (GDPR/UK GDPR) obtained through Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP). If you decline consent, we show contextual or limited ads (or reduce tracking) when available. In California, we honor “Do Not Sell or Share” choices under CPRA via AdMob’s privacy controls.

Your choices

  • Consent (EEA/UK): You’ll see a consent form on first launch, and periodically if Google requires it.
  • Change your choice: In the app’s Settings, tap Privacy → Manage Ads Consent to re-open the consent screen (if your current app version supports it). You can also adjust iOS settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Limit Ad Tracking: iOS → Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising / Tracking.

Data retention

Ad vendors retain data per their policies to provide reporting, fraud prevention, and regulatory compliance. We do not store raw ad identifiers in our databases.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the local age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Updates

We may update this page as we add or remove ad partners or change settings. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
